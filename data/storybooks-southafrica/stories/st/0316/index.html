Ke rata ho tlola kgathi le motswalle wa ka.

I enjoy skipping with my friend.

Ke rata ho ngola ditlhaku letlapeng.

I enjoy writing letters on the board.

Ke rata ho bala dibuka tse nang le ditshwantsho.

I enjoy reading books with pictures.

Ke rata ho ja ditholwana tse fapaneng.

I enjoy eating different fruit.

Ke rata ho thusa mona hae.

I enjoy helping at home.

Ke rata ho mamela dipale tsa Mme.

I enjoy listening to my motherâ€™s stories.

Ke rata ho robala betheng e kgolo.

I enjoy sleeping on a big bed.

Ke rata ho etsa dintho tsena. Ke eng seo o ratang ho se etsa?

I enjoy doing these things. What do you enjoy doing?

Written by: Ursula Nafula
Illustrated by: Wiehan de Jager
Translated by: Tseliso Masolane
Language: Sesotho
Level: Level 1
